Soraya Zidane

Soraya and I met at Playa ddel Carmen in 2021. Soraya opened her business at that year, and experienced great sucess. Over time she felt that her target audience was not the target audience she was alming for in the first place, and the nature of the services she wanted to offer varied as she became smarter and more experienced. Together we set some new basic values to her business.


Brand Strategy



At first we had define what services Soraya was interested in providing. What products does she offer her customer. So far it has mainly, provided personal training services to male business men.


As we delved deeper into the goals of the business, we realized that Soraya in general feels much more suited to serving women. If so it was necessary to change the message she sends to her clients from a more masculine message to a neutral message that would attract women as well.


The third phase was to determine Soraya’s firm brand positioning within the professional services marketplace. What makes her different and what added value does she bring to her customers?


Brand Identidy



The colors


We picked Soraya four brand colors which will be the base for all of our new design.



The Logo


Soraya had her great logo, which we decided to keep, but with a few rules.


Since we decided that Soraya wants to offer her services to three types of customers, we decided that each type of service will have its own color in order to maintain uniformity and help the customer to flex.

The Typography and tone


We set together a font that conveys Soraya’s tome- Friendly and energetic.


When we have a good base of product definition and goal setting, it is very easy to find the product language that will connect to the general concept we want to convey to customers in the end, we not only want more customers we also want customers to connect best to our product.


The Pictures


It is of great importance to choose the images for our brand.
“A picture is worth a thousand words”.
This is another way to introduce our potential customers to details about our services.


In this case we chose to present pictures of a Soraya happy, practicing, in nature – all the things she would like to convey to her clients as key values for success in the program.

The Graphic Elements


The last thing Soraya needed was Graphic elements in order to finish the design lines on her website and publishing. We made the choice of elements by brainstorming the shapes and words that connect to its brand. From our results we created abstract elements and vector.
